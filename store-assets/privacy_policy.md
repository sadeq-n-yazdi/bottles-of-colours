# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 17, 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to the puzzle application (the "App") published on Google Play. The App is free and open-source software, licensed under the [GNU General Public License v3.0](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.html).

The short version: **the App collects nothing about you. Full stop.**

---

## Information We Collect

**None.**

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or any other information from or about you or your device.

There are no:

- User accounts or registration
- Analytics or crash reporting tools
- Advertising SDKs or ad networks
- Tracking pixels or fingerprinting mechanisms
- Cookies or persistent identifiers
- Location, camera, microphone, or contact access
- Network requests of any kind

The App works entirely offline. No data ever leaves your device as a result of using the App.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, services, or APIs. No data is shared with any third party because no data is collected in the first place.

---

## Permissions

The App requests no Android permissions. It does not access any sensitive device features.

## Children's Privacy
