Bottle of Colors is a calm, satisfying water-sort puzzle. Each flask holds a stack of brightly colored liquids. Tap a source flask, then a destination flask to pour — but only if the destination is empty or its top color matches the source. Sort every flask into a single pure color, and you win.

Simple to learn, surprisingly deep to master. Perfect for short breaks, long commutes, or unwinding before sleep.

WHAT'S INSIDE

• 100 hand-tuned levels with a smooth difficulty curve
   - Start with 4 bottles and 4 slots
   - Work your way up to 12 bottles, 7 slots, 10 colors
   - Each level uses a fixed layout, so you can compare moves with friends

• Multiple player profiles
   - Pass the phone between family members
   - Each player has their own saved progress
   - Big "Play" button on the home screen jumps you straight to your next unsolved level
   - "Change player" anytime from the home screen

• Smart undo
   - Every 10 moves you earn 1 undo
   - Hold up to 3 undos at once
   - Stuck? Use one to back out and try a different approach

• Unsolvable detection
   - If no valid moves remain, the game tells you immediately
   - Offers Undo, Reset, or Back so you never get silently stuck

• Custom puzzles
   - Dial in your own difficulty: 3-12 bottles, 2-8 slots per bottle, 1-3 empty bottles
   - New random layout every time
   - Great for warming up or chasing a specific challenge

• Polished feel
   - High-contrast 10-color palette designed so adjacent liquids are easy to tell apart
   - Smooth pour animations: the source bottle tilts, colors fade out at the top and fade in at the destination
   - Cork cap pops onto a flask the moment it's fully sorted
   - Sparkle burst celebrates every solved bottle
   - Subtle haptic feedback on every tap, pour, and win

• Built to fit your screen
   - The board automatically scales to fit every flask in view
   - No scrolling, no zooming — works on small phones and tablets

DESIGNED FOR FOCUS

No ads. No pop-ups. No timers, lives, or stars to chase. No social pressure. Just you, the flasks, and your next move. Take your time, undo when you need to, and enjoy a moment of calm sorting.

HOW TO PLAY

1. Tap a flask to pick it as the source — it lifts up and gets an amber outline.
2. Tap a second flask to pour into it.
3. A pour is valid if the destination is empty, or its top color matches the source's top color.
4. All contiguous units of the source's top color transfer at once, as long as the destination has room.
5. Solve every flask: each one must be either empty, or completely full of a single color.

That's it. Whether you finish a level in 6 moves or 60, the puzzle is the same. The only score that matters is the satisfying click of the cork popping into place.
